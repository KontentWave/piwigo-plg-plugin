Who should use this plugin?

Profile Liveness Guard is for gallery administrators who need recurring proof that a profile owner still controls the verified phone number tied to a root album.

What it monitors

  • Recurring liveness checks for non-admin album owners
  • Manual or due-scan SMS challenge delivery
  • Grace-period tracking for unconfirmed challenges
  • Automatic album-tree privatization after expiry
  • Administrator-led visibility restoration and audit review

Operational note

LanguageSwitch can switch PLG into any installed Piwigo locale. This plugin now ships dedicated PLG translations for English (UK), Spanish, Hungarian, Slovak, Russian, Ukrainian, and Simplified Chinese.
